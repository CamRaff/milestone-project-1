THIS IS BARCA

BARCA is one of the most beloved spots in Tynemouth, a quaint little seaside town in the North East of England. BARCA is a quirky little bar with a lot of character. Boasting over 10 draught beers and a bespoke cocktail list, BARCA holds a special place in the hearts of many native to the area!
