BARCA Drinks

Below is a short preview of the kind of drinks we have on offer here at BARCA. Please be aware that the drinks on offer may change, and that you must have valid proof of ID to make a purchase!

Two By Two

Two By Two is a brewery location in Wallsend. They have worked tirelessly since their inception to create some absolutely top tier beverages, and this one is no different! Here we have their offering "Snake Eyes", boasting a very modest 4.7% ABV, this single hopped ale is a fan favourite!

£6 per pint!

Suitable for Vegans!

Guinness

If you haven't heard of Guinness, where have you been all of your life?! Guinness is Ireland's extremely famous stout, and beloved all around the world! Guinness is an easy 4.2% ABV, making it perfect for a session beer. Over 260 years in the making, you do not want to miss out on a freshly poured Guinness!

£5 per pint!

Suitable for Vegans!

Camden Hells

Camden Town Brewery have smashed it out of the park with this cross between a Helles and a Pilsner, brewed with malt and hops freshly out of Germany! It is a very classic and crisp beverage, and being only 4.6% ABV, you could drink them all day long!

£5.50 per pint!

Suitable for Vegans!
